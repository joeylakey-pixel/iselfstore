About Us

Independent. Local.
Trusted.

iSelfStore is an independent self-storage business based in South Hykeham, Lincoln. We designed and built our facility from the ground up with one goal: to provide the most modern, secure, and affordable self-storage experience in Lincoln.

With 65 self-service storage units across three sizes, our site is equipped with anti-condensation insulation, 24/7 CCTV monitoring, and unrestricted drive-in access every day of the year. Conveniently located just off the A46 Lincoln bypass.
